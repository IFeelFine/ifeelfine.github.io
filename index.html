Inspired by Open Source Software

We love the principles of Free and Open Source Software and make our solutions available for everyone to use.

We take much of our inspiration for our Docker projects from the folks at LinuxServer.io. Their Docker images are world class and have been an invaluable learning tool. Please visit and support them.

Reusable Images

In school, you're told you cannot copy someone else's work, but as usual the real world is different. Being told you're wasting valuable company time by not building on what came before is the fear of all fresh recruits.

We use the s6-overlay in each of our Docker images, making them configurable for practically any application. Using Alpine Linux, we keep solutions small enough for even the most space conscious client.

First-class Documentation

Projects are only as good as their documentation, especially when looking back after a number of months or even years. Our projects are thoroughly documented for ease of use. Some of these can be found on GitHub. Additional guides and musings can also also be found on our blog.
